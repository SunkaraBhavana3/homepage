About SD College of Information Technology

Established in 2001, SD College of Information Technology (SDCIT) in Tanuku, Andhra Pradesh, is a co-educational institution managed by the Sri Venkata Krishna Educational Society. Initially affiliated with Andhra University, the college transitioned to Adikavi Nannaya University in 2016. SDCIT is dedicated to providing quality education in business administration and computer applications, aiming to nurture future innovators and leaders.

Our Esteemed Programs

Our MBA program is designed to cultivate future leaders with a strong foundation in business strategy, finance, marketing, and management. Emphasizing practical skills and real-world case studies, graduates are prepared for executive roles across various industries.
